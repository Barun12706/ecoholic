Why Us

Problem Statement

Patna’s commercial establishments generate large volumes of waste daily. Most waste is mixed, sent to landfill or dumping sites, and results in pollution, health risks, and loss of valuable recyclable materials. Businesses face increasing pressure to comply with Plastic Waste Management Rules and EPR, but lack a transparent, reliable digital system.

What’s broken today

Low segregation quality and poor traceability
No live compliance reporting for audits
Recyclable value leaked or lost

Our Solution

Smart collection QR-coded bins + app-based pickup scans create verified records of quantity, category, and time.
High-quality segregation Organic, recyclable plastic, dry waste, and used cooking oil separated at source.
Resource recovery Organic waste becomes compost and bio-feedstock. Plastics are supplied to authorized recyclers.
Compliance reporting Automated dashboards and certificates for EPR and audit-ready reporting.

Innovation & Technology

Mobile apps for customers, collectors, and admins
QR scanning for every pickup
Live dashboard with ward-wise data
Route optimization to reduce fuel and time
Carbon credit tracking + EPR certificate generation

What makes it unique

We combine physical operations with digital traceability and circular monetization, making the model replicable across small and medium cities.
